Eclipse addons required for NetXMS development:

* Draw2D: http://download.eclipse.org/tools/gef/updates/legacy/releases/
* NetXMS Zest fork: http://www.netxms.org/eclipse/zest-rcp
* Properties Editor: http://propedit.sourceforge.jp/eclipse/updates
* "Target Management Terminal" from "Target Management Project 4.4". Update site: http://download.eclipse.org/tm/terminal/updates/4.4milestones/20180611
* BIRT Charting: http://download.eclipse.org/birt/update-site/4.4/
* Babel Project: http://download.eclipse.org/technology/babel/update-site/R0.14.1/mars
	Required locales: chinese (both), czech, french, german, russian, portuguese, spanish
